Our Story

65+ Years of Sweetness
Three Generations Strong

Founded in 1960, M. Mangilal Sweets has been the heartbeat of Narsipatnam's culinary heritage for over six decades. What began as a humble sweets counter has grown into the town's most beloved destination — trusted by three generations of families for festivals, celebrations, and everyday indulgence.

Our commitment remains unchanged since day one — pure ghee, authentic recipes, and no shortcuts. Today, our modern outlet blends that timeless artistry with contemporary excellence — gleaming counters, a spotless kitchen, and a warm dining space that carries forward a legacy built on taste, trust, and tradition.
